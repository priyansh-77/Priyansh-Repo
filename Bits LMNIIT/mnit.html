MODE OF ADMISSION

One needs to take the JEE (Main) exam.

One also needs to secure at least 75% marks (65% for OBC-NCL, 65% for Defence, 50% for SC/ST/PwD) in aggregate of Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics in Class XII.

Admissions are done on the basis of rank in JEE (Main) through JOSAA and csab Counselling.
